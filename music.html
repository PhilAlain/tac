Connecting Marshall Speaker

How to connect and pair

1. Select Bluetooth as the input

Pushing the SOURCE button toggles the input sources.

2. Push and hold the SOURCE button for 2 seconds

The Bluetooth indicator starts to blink.

3. Enable Bluetooth on your device (Smartphone, Tablet, Computer)

You should find a device called Stanmore II or similar name to pair.

4. Select Stanmore II from the Bluetooth list and accept pairing.

You should find a device called Stanmore II or similar name to pair.

Stanmore II Bluetooth can be connected to two Bluetooth devices at the same time. Playing a connected Bluetooth device will make it the selected audio source, pausing any previously played audio on the second device.

Let the music play !
