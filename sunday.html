Sunday Morning

A Vibrant Local Tradition

Sunday mornings:

On Sunday mornings, and occasionally on other special days, the nearby "MAS: Maison des Associations" hosts Gospel masses.

These events often draw a lively, diverse crowd to the street, creating a - sometimes surprising - dynamic atmosphere in the neighborhood.

On warm days, you can enjoy the soulful gospel melodies in the courtyard.

On warm days, the soulful melodies often drift into the courtyard.
